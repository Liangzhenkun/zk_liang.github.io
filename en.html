Publications

2024

AgentSense: Benchmarking Social Intelligence of Language Agents through Interactive Scenarios Preprint

Bruce Liang*, Jingcong Liang*, Jiayu Lin*, Xinnong Zhang*, Xiawei Liu, Shiyue Yang, Rong Ye, Lei Chen, Haoyu Kuang, Xuanjing Huang, Zhongyu Wei.

PDF Code Dataset Demo

This paper presents a benchmarking framework for evaluating the social intelligence of language agents through interactive scenarios. The main contributions include designing a series of interactive scenarios, establishing multi-dimensional evaluation metrics, and analyzing the capabilities and limitations of current language models in terms of social intelligence.

2024

Unveiling the Truth and Facilitating Change: Towards Agent-based Large-scale Social Movement Simulation ACL-Findings

Bruce Liang, Zhongyu Wei, Xuanjing Huang.

PDF Code

This research explores agent-based methods for large-scale social movement simulation, proposing a new framework to reveal the truth in social movements and facilitate change. We built a multi-agent system capable of simulating large-scale social interactions and information dissemination processes, providing a new computational approach to understanding social dynamics.

2023

Exploring Applications and Ethical Challenges of Large Language Models in Social Science Research Journal

Bruce Liang, Someone Else, Another Author.

PDF

This paper provides a comprehensive analysis of the application prospects of large language models in social science research, while deeply exploring related ethical challenges and solutions. The study points out the potential of language models in analyzing social phenomena, simulating human behavior, and assisting in theory construction, and proposes methods to mitigate potential biases and ensure research ethics.
